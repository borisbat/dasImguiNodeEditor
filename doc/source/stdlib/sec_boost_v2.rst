**********************************
Boost v2 — the node-editor DSL
**********************************

The DSL spine of dasImguiNodeEditor: the graph-entity macros (``node_editor``,
``node``, ``pin``, ``link``, ``node_group``), the queue-injectable link-creation
and item-deletion protocols, selection, context-menu and shortcut events,
per-scope styling, and link-flow animation — all bracketed by an app-owned
``EditorContext?``.
